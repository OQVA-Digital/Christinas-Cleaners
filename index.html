About Christina's Cleaners

Christina's Cleaners is a unique fusion of top-tier professional cleaning services and social responsibility. Based in Oxfordshire, we are driven by our mission to empower Ukrainian female refugees to rebuild their lives while delivering exceptional cleaning services to our clients.

Our journey began with a vision - to provide reliable, high-quality domestic cleaning services and make a positive social impact. Our team comprises resilient, dedicated, and highly-skilled women from Ukraine, who not only work hard to meet your cleaning needs but are also overcoming adversity and building a better future for themselves in the UK.

Located in Oxfordshire, we serve various areas including Headington, Cowley, Marston, Botley, and beyond, delivering consistent, high-quality cleaning services while making a difference.
