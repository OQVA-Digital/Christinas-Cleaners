About Christina's Cleaners

Christina's Cleaners is a socially conscious cleaning company in Oxfordshire, committed to providing exceptional cleaning services and making a difference in the lives of female Ukrainian refugees. Our team, primarily composed of women who have faced tremendous challenges, is fully insured and trained to deliver outstanding results. By choosing our services, you contribute to their social and economic integration in the UK.

At Christina's Cleaners, we believe in giving back to society and making a real difference. Learn more about the impact we're making in the lives of female Ukrainian refugees and how your support helps them regain stability, learn English, and embrace their new life in the UK.
