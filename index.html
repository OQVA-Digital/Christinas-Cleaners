Our services

At Christina's Cleaners, we understand that every home is unique and has different cleaning needs. That's why we offer tailored domestic cleaning services to suit your schedule and requirements. Whether you're looking for regular weekly cleaning, need an emergency clean on the same day, or require a comprehensive end-of-tenancy clean, our highly-skilled, dedicated team is ready to deliver exceptional results.

Standard Cleaning

Keeping your home clean on a regular basis can be challenging amidst the hustle and bustle of daily life. That's where we step in. Christina's Cleaners offers a comprehensive Standard Cleaning service, covering all the basics and maintaining cleanliness and hygiene in your home.

Our Standard Cleaning service includes dusting, vacuuming, mopping, kitchen cleaning, bathroom cleaning, and general tidying. We focus on high-traffic areas, ensuring they're always inviting and fresh. This service is perfect for those looking for a regular cleaning schedule, so you can focus on what matters most to you, while we take care of the tidying up.

Deep Cleaning

Every once in a while, your home needs a little extra attention. Our Deep Cleaning service is perfect for those moments. We dig a little deeper, reaching those often-neglected areas to ensure your home is spotless from top to bottom.

In addition to the services offered in our Standard Cleaning, our Deep Cleaning includes the cleaning of oven interiors, inside windows, under furniture, detailed dusting and more. End of Tenancy Cleaning

About Christina's Cleaners

Christina's Cleaners is a unique fusion of top-tier professional cleaning services and social responsibility. Based in Oxfordshire, we are driven by our mission to empower Ukrainian female refugees to rebuild their lives while delivering exceptional cleaning services to our clients.

Our journey began with a vision - to provide reliable, high-quality domestic cleaning services and make a positive social impact. Our team comprises resilient, dedicated, and highly-skilled women from Ukraine, who not only work hard to meet your cleaning needs but are also overcoming adversity and building a better future for themselves in the UK.

Located in Oxfordshire, we serve various areas including Headington, Cowley, Marston, Botley, and beyond, delivering consistent, high-quality cleaning services while making a difference.
